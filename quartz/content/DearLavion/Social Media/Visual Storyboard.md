### ✅ **Storyboard Notes**

1. **Duration:** Each Reel/Story ~15–30 seconds.
    
2. **Branding:** Keep **colors, fonts, and animation style consistent**.
    
3. **Animation Style:**
    
    - Dotted paths for guidance.
        
    - Glow or pop-up effects for solutions and highlights.
        
    - Subtle shake or zoom for pain points.
        
4. **Text Overlays:** Short, punchy, easy to read in **2–3 seconds per line**.
    
5. **Music / Sounds:** Use **soft upbeat music** for solutions/emotions, **slightly tense music** for pain points.
    
6. **Stickers / CTA:** Polls for engagement, hearts for emotional moments, “Join Today” for conversion.
