## **1️⃣ Core Social Media Strategy**

### **Goals**

- **Awareness:** Introduce Dear Lavion as the _go-to app for travel help & small wishes_.
    
- **Engagement:** Encourage users to share travel frustrations, stories, and wishes.
    
- **Conversion:** Drive downloads/sign-ups for Wishers and Copilots.
    

### **Target Audience**

- Travelers aged 18–35 (solo travelers, backpackers, urban explorers).
    
- Tech-savvy people looking for **stress-free travel solutions**.
    
- Socially conscious people who enjoy **helping others or receiving help**.
    

### **Content Pillars**

1. **Pain Points:** Highlight common travel struggles (lost, crowded areas, language barriers, luggage, missed events).
    
2. **Solutions:** Show how Copilots make life easier and fun.
    
3. **Emotions:** Heartwarming stories of kindness, friendship, and travel happiness.
    
4. **Social Proof:** Showcase user testimonials, numbers of wishes granted, and community impact.
    
5. **Tips & Hacks:** Travel tips, micro-guides, and seasonal hacks.
    
6. **Engagement Posts:** Polls, “Would you…?”, or choose-your-adventure style posts.
