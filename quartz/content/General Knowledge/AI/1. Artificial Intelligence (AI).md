
### **What**

> **AI is the broad field**, ability of machines to mimic human intelligence: such as reasoning, decision-making, and understanding language.

### **Why**

> To **automate decision-making**, reduce human effort, and solve complex problems at scale.

### **How**

> Through rules, algorithms, and data-driven models—including machine learning.

# One-Line Differentiation (Very Interview-Friendly)

- **AI:** Thinks like a human
- **ML:** Learns from past data
- **DL:** Learns deeply using neural networks
- **GenAI:** Creates new content
- **LLM:** Understands and generates language
